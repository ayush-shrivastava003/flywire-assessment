# Further reading

Below is further reading suggested by Exodiac:

* **Whole-Brain Cell Typing:** *Schlegel et al., 2024* ("Whole-brain annotation and multi-connectome cell typing of Drosophila") provides the definitive catalog and nomenclature for FlyWire.
* **Mushroom Body (KCs, MBONs, PAM/PPL):** *Aso et al., 2014* ("The neuronal organization of the Drosophila mushroom body") defines the anatomy, transmitter profiles, and valence-coding of these circuits.
* **Central Complex (FS3, hDelta, FB6A):** *Hulse et al., 2021* ("A connectome of the Drosophila central complex") details the navigation, heading, and sleep networks.
* **Visual Motion & Projection (Mi4, Mi9, Tm1, Tm3, T5, LC10):** *Shinomiya et al., 2019* (optic lobe connectome) and *Borst et al., 2020* explain the ON/OFF direction-selective circuits.
* **Descending Pathways (DNg, DNp):** *Namiki et al., 2018* ("The functional organization of descending sensory-motor pathways in Drosophila") maps the descending motor control networks.
* **Courtship (mAL):** *Kallman et al., 2015* or *Yu et al., 2010* cover these sexually dimorphic behavior circuits.
* **Auditory/Mechanosensory (JO-A/B):** *Kamikouchi et al., 2009* maps Johnston's organ projections and functional segregation.
